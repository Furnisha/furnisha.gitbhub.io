About Us

Welcome to Man Like Politics, a political pocast produced by three London-based millenials: Pouneh Ahari, Camarley McIntosh and Furnisha Lycett. Having met at university in 2011, Furnisha, Pouneh and Marley have since developed a strong friendship based on passionate discussion. Originating from three diverse backgrounds, the trio bring unique and insightful views to all aspects of politics, exploring both current and historic topics in an insightful and compelling way.

Furnisha Lycett

Born and bred Londoner, Furnisha is a Consultant currently working in the financial services sector. Having read Geography at university and having previously worked in the technology industry, Furnisha has a keen interest in the intersection between technology, the environment and people. Her interest in politics grew during her time at university where she was influenced by Pouneh's determination for change and her sharing of ideas.
